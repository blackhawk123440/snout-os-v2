# Copy Style Guide

Enterprise tone for Snout OS. Direct, precise, calm.

## Voice Principles

- **Direct**: Say what you mean. No hedging or filler.
- **Precise**: Use exact terms. Avoid vague language.
- **Calm**: No urgency theater. No exclamation-heavy tone.

## Error Messages

- Be actionable. Tell the user what to do next.
- Examples:
  - "Connection failed. Retry?"
  - "Unable to load. Check your connection and try again."
  - "Invalid date range. Start must be before end."

## Empty States

- Contextual and non-cute.
- No emojis, no playful language.
- Examples:
  - "No payouts yet. Transfers will appear after completed bookings."
  - "No visits today. Check Calendar for upcoming."
  - "No reports yet. Your sitter will send updates after each visit."

## Forbidden

- Exclamation-heavy tone
- Emojis in UI copy
- Playful or cutesy language
- "Oops", "Whoops", "Yay"
- Overly enthusiastic CTAs ("Get started now!")

## Preferred

- Short, declarative sentences
- "View bookings" not "See your bookings!"
- "Refresh" not "Refresh now"
- "Complete" not "All done!"
